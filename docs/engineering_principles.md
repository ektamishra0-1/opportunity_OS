# Engineering Principles — Opportunity OS

## 1. Real Data Only

No synthetic or dummy datasets in production logic.

---

## 2. Evidence First

Every problem must be backed by observations.

---

## 3. No Single-Source Truth

All insights must be derived from multiple sources.

---

## 4. Strict Agent Boundaries

Each agent has one responsibility only.

---

## 5. Observations Are Core Memory

Raw observations are never discarded.

---

## 6. Separation of Discovery and Invention

- Discovery = detect problems
- Invention = generate solutions

---

## 7. Domain-Driven Architecture

Everything starts from a domain, not a fixed dataset.

---

## 8. Human-in-the-Loop Decisions

System informs, but does not decide business outcomes.

---

## 9. Continuous Learning

System improves source quality and clustering over time.

---

## 10. No Monolithic Intelligence

Intelligence is distributed across agents, not centralized.
