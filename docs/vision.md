# Vision

Most people search for startup ideas.

Opportunity OS searches for problems.

The platform continuously discovers, analyzes, and ranks real-world pain points across industries.

Users can explore opportunities in domains such as:

- Biotech
- Healthcare
- AI
- Education
- Finance
- Manufacturing

The system focuses on understanding:

- Who experiences the problem
- How severe the problem is
- Existing solutions
- Market gaps
- AI suitability
- Startup potential

The goal is to become a search engine for opportunities.
